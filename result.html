How We Collect Our Data

At InsightReport, we believe in being open about where our information comes from. This page shows you the actual survey questions we asked and how people answered them. This data helped us write our reports about jobs at risk and online earning.

Our research team, led by Xavier Baron, conducted online surveys with over 13,000 people across 35 countries. We made sure to include people from different age groups, jobs, and regions to get a complete picture of global trends.

Below you'll see the exact questions we asked and the percentage of people who chose each answer. This raw data is what we used to find patterns and draw conclusions in our main reports.

How This Data Helps

The survey results you see above helped us understand real experiences and concerns of workers around the world. When we noticed that 25% of workers expect significant job changes due to technology, we knew this was an important trend to explore further.

Similarly, seeing that 45% of online earners use it as extra money alongside regular jobs showed us how digital work is becoming a normal part of people's income strategies.

By sharing this raw data, we hope to give you a clearer picture of how we reach our conclusions. You can see exactly what people told us and draw your own insights from the numbers.

Our Research Process

Our research team, led by Xavier Baron, follows a careful process:

  • First, we identify important topics people want to know about
  • Next, we create survey questions that are easy to understand
  • We then share these surveys with diverse groups of people
  • After collecting responses, we analyze the patterns in the data
  • Finally, we share both the raw data and our analysis with you

We believe that being open about our data sources makes our reports more trustworthy and helpful. If you have questions about our research methods, please don't hesitate to contact us.
